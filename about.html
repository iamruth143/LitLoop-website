About Lit Loop

Rent Books, Not Shelves — Based in Guntur, AP

Lit Loop is a modern book rental service that makes reading affordable and accessible. We deliver books to your doorstep and pick them up when you're done — no subscriptions, no hassle, just great books at a fraction of the price.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to great books without the burden of ownership. Lit Loop was born from a simple idea: why buy a book you'll read once when you can rent it for a fraction of the cost?

Our mission is to create a community of readers who share, discover, and enjoy books together — affordably and sustainably.

What We Do

Lit Loop provides a seamless book rental experience through our mobile app:

  • Browse & Discover — Explore our growing catalogue of 500+ books across 50+ genres, from fiction and non-fiction to academic and self-help.
  • Rent Affordably — Starting at just ₹6/day, our rental prices are a fraction of what you'd pay to buy.
  • Doorstep Delivery — Choose home or college delivery, set your address via Google Maps, and we'll bring the book to you.
  • Easy Returns — When you're done reading, schedule a pickup and we'll collect the book right from your doorstep.
  • Flexible Payments — Pay via UPI or cash, whichever works for you.

Where We Operate

Lit Loop is currently available in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, India. We started here because we know and love this city — and we're working hard to expand to more cities across India soon.

Whether you're a college student at a local campus or a book lover at home, if you're in Guntur, we've got you covered.

Why Choose Lit Loop?

  • Save Money — Rent for ₹6/day instead of paying ₹200–₹800 to buy.
  • Zero Clutter — No more dusty bookshelves; return books when you're done.
  • Discover More — With affordable rentals, you can explore genres you wouldn't normally buy.
  • Eco-Friendly — Sharing books means fewer books printed and wasted.
  • Real-Time Tracking — Know exactly where your delivery is, every step of the way.
