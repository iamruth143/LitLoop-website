Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Lit Loop

Got questions? We've got answers. Browse the most common questions about our book rental service below. If you can't find what you're looking for, contact us and we'll help.

General

Lit Loop is a book rental service based in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh. We let you rent physical books at affordable prices (starting ₹6/day) with free doorstep delivery and pickup.

We currently deliver in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, India.

Download the Lit Loop app, tap Sign Up, and create your free account with your name, email, and phone number. No credit card required.

Pricing & Payments

Rental prices start at just ₹6 per day, with no subscription or hidden fees. The total cost depends on how many days you keep the book. Pricing for each book is shown in the app.

We accept UPI payments (via QR code scan in apps like GPay, PhonePe, Paytm) and Cash on delivery/pickup. No credit card or subscription is needed.

Lit Loop verifies customer identity before completing a rental. A small security amount may be collected only when required. If fake identity details or fraudulent rental activity are found, Lit Loop may take necessary action to recover the book, rental dues, or replacement cost.

Delivery & Returns

After you place a rental request in the app, a convenient delivery time is scheduled. Books are delivered right to your doorstep — whether that's your home or college. You'll get real-time updates on your delivery status.

When you're done reading, open the app and schedule a pickup. The book will be collected from your doorstep at the agreed time. Free of charge.

The book is checked in front of you during delivery and pickup. If you notice any damage at delivery, report it immediately so it can be recorded before your rental starts.

Damage charges depend on the severity of the damage. If the book is lost, misplaced, or damaged so badly that it cannot be rented again, the customer must pay the replacement cost of the book.

Book damage includes stains, torn pages, water damage, oil or curry spills, writing or markings, missing pages, damaged covers, or any condition that reduces the book's rental quality. A minimum damage charge of ₹30 may be collected depending on the issue.

College students must show a valid college ID. The person receiving the book must be the same person whose ID was submitted for verification.

You can cancel a rental request before delivery is scheduled by contacting support. Once delivery is scheduled, cancellations are at Lit Loop's discretion and may incur a fee.
